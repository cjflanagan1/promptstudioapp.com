Save every prompt to your personal library. Organize with folders and category tags, search instantly, and access your full version history.

The Vault intelligently retrieves relevant passages from your documents and injects them into your prompt — automatically or with manual file selection.

Every edit creates a new version. Browse your full prompt history, compare changes, and roll back anytime. Nothing is ever lost.
